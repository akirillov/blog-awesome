About this blog

  • The main purpose of this blog is knowledge sharing. Everyone knows how hard it is to find proper documentation or how-to appropriate for one’s needs. Dealing with emerging software domains, complex systems or bleeding edge technologies requires knowledge exchange for growth of communities, faster problem solving and of course better understanding of the field by the author
  • The blog is focused mainly on distributed systems and functional programming. But how-tos are also published just for having a manual near at hand and not to bury useful information in corporate wikis - knowledge opensourcing
  • Why octopus? In my opinion octopus is a great metaphor for a distributed system: it could perform multiple heterogenous tasks in parallel in asynchronous manner. GitHub’s octocat also influenced this metaphor a lot. Why is it flying? Because flying is fast and a good system must be fast: have low latencies and high throughput rate
